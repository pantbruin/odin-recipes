Chicken Gyro

Description

A Greek-inspired Mediterranean chicken gyro. A healthy meal that is quick and easy to make!

  • Dried Oregano
  • Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breast
  • 6 inch Pita Bread Rounds
  • Diced Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Iceberg lettuce
